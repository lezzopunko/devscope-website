Classic Banana Bread

Making delicious banana bread is incredibly simple and a perfect way to use up overripe bananas.

Key Ingredients
  • • 3 overripe bananas, mashed
  • • 1/3 cup melted butter
  • • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • • 1 pinch of salt
  • • 3/4 cup sugar
  • • 1 large egg, beaten
  • • 1 1/2 cups of all-purpose flour
Simple Baking Steps
  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a standard loaf pan.
  2. In a mixing bowl, mash the ripe bananas with a fork until completely smooth.
  3. Stir the melted butter into the mashed bananas until well combined.
  4. Mix in the baking soda, salt, sugar, beaten egg, and flour until the batter is uniform.
  5. Pour into your pan and bake for 50 to 60 minutes until a toothpick comes out clean.
